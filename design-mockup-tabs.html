How the tab model works
  • Click any top-nav item → it opens (or focuses) a tab below. Your other tabs stay exactly where you left them — no losing a half-filled booking form because you peeked at the Calendar.
  • Tabs are closable (×) and the first one is pinned. A "+" spawns a fresh tab.
  • Real version would carry context — e.g. a tab titled "Booking #1042 · Sharma" rather than just "Bookings" — and could persist across reloads.
  • Built entirely on your existing tokens; no new UI library required for this layout.
